Fast Drain Cleaning When You Need It Most

A blocked or slow drain is one of the most common plumbing issues homeowners across the Tri-Cities face. Whether it's a kitchen sink that won't empty, a shower that backs up, or a main sewer line emergency, our licensed plumbers respond fast across Coquitlam, Port Moody, and Port Coquitlam.

We handle every type of drain blockage — from minor clogs caused by hair and soap scum to serious main line issues caused by tree roots, grease buildup, or collapsed pipes. Our team uses commercial-grade equipment to clear drains thoroughly the first time.

Drain Cleaning Services We Offer

Kitchen Drain Cleaning

Kitchen drains often clog due to grease, food particles, and soap residue. We clear blockages using professional-grade snakes and hydro-jetting equipment to restore full flow.

Bathroom Drain Cleaning

Hair, toothpaste, and soap scum are the main culprits in bathroom drain blockages. We clear shower, tub, and bathroom sink drains quickly without damage to your fixtures.

Main Sewer Line Cleaning

When multiple drains back up at once, the issue is usually in your main sewer line. Common causes include tree root intrusion, collapsed pipes, or major buildup. We use camera inspection and hydro-jetting to identify and clear the problem.

Floor Drain Cleaning

Basement and laundry room floor drains require regular maintenance, especially in older Tri-Cities homes. We service and clean all floor drain types.

Outdoor Drain Cleaning

Storm drains and outdoor catch basins can get blocked by leaves and debris. We clear and inspect outdoor drainage systems before fall and spring rain seasons.

Common Tri-Cities Drain Issues

Older homes throughout Coquitlam and Port Moody often have aging pipes that are more prone to blockages. We see this especially in the Heritage Mountain area, Inlet Centre, and central Coquitlam neighbourhoods. Tree root intrusion is also a frequent cause given the mature trees in our region.

Signs You Need Professional Drain Cleaning

  • Water draining slowly from sinks, tubs, or showers
  • Gurgling sounds from drains or toilets
  • Foul odours coming from drains
  • Multiple fixtures backing up simultaneously
  • Water pooling around floor drains
  • Sewage backups in basement or lower levels
  • Toilets that flush sluggishly or back up

If you notice any of these signs, call us before the issue worsens. A small clog left untreated can become a major sewer line problem.
