Find Hidden Leaks Before They Cause Major Damage

Hidden water leaks are silent destroyers. They can damage flooring, walls, foundations, and personal property — and they often go undetected until major damage appears. Our leak detection specialists use advanced technology to locate leaks across Coquitlam, Port Moody, and Port Coquitlam with minimal disruption to your home.

Leak Detection Services

Slab Leak Detection

Many Tri-Cities homes have water lines running under concrete slabs. Slab leaks are tricky to find but our specialized equipment locates them precisely without unnecessary jackhammering.

Wall Leak Detection

Pipes inside walls can leak silently for months. We use thermal imaging and moisture meters to identify the exact leak location before any drywall is removed.

Ceiling Leak Detection

Water on a ceiling rarely originates directly above the visible spot — it travels along joists. We trace water back to its source accurately.

Underground Leak Detection

Main water line leaks between the meter and house can waste enormous amounts of water. We use acoustic listening equipment and tracer gas to locate underground leaks.

Pool and Pool Plumbing Leaks

If your pool is losing water faster than evaporation accounts for, we can identify whether the issue is in the pool shell or the plumbing system.

Sewer and Drain Leaks

Sewer leaks are environmental and structural concerns. We use camera inspection to find cracks, broken joints, and root intrusion in drain lines.

Signs You May Have a Hidden Leak

• Sudden increase in water bill without explanation
• Damp or warm spots on floors
• Musty or mold-like smells
• Sound of running water when nothing is on
• Discoloured walls, ceiling, or flooring
• Foundation cracks (new or worsening)
• Mildew growth in unusual places
• Lawn areas that stay green or wet

Our Leak Detection Technology

Modern leak detection isn't guesswork. We use:

  • Acoustic detection: Specialized microphones that hear water escaping pipes through walls and floors
  • Thermal imaging cameras: Detect temperature differences caused by water leaks
  • Moisture meters: Pinpoint elevated moisture in walls, floors, and ceilings
  • Pressure testing: Identify which section of plumbing is leaking
  • Tracer gas: For underground and slab leak detection
  • Camera inspection: Internal pipe inspection for drain and sewer issues

Why Early Detection Saves Thousands

The cost of finding a leak early is dramatically less than dealing with major water damage later. A typical hidden leak can:

  • Damage flooring, drywall, and structural elements
  • Promote mold and mildew growth (health hazard)
  • Increase water bills by hundreds per month
  • Compromise foundations and structural integrity
  • Reduce your home's value
