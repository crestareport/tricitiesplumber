Expert Toilet and Faucet Service Across the Tri-Cities

Running toilets and dripping faucets seem minor but waste thousands of litres of water annually and add up on your utility bill. Our licensed plumbers handle all types of toilet and faucet repairs across Coquitlam, Port Moody, and Port Coquitlam, usually with same-day service.

Toilet Repair Services

Running Toilet Repair

A running toilet can waste over 750 litres per day. Common causes include flapper valve failure, fill valve issues, and float adjustment problems. Most running toilet repairs are completed in under an hour.

Toilet Won't Flush

Weak flushing is usually caused by clogged jet holes, low water level, or a failing fill valve. We diagnose and fix all flushing issues.

Toilet Leaks

Leaks at the base of a toilet typically indicate a failing wax seal or loose flange. We replace seals and reinstall toilets properly to prevent water damage to flooring.

Toilet Replacement

Old toilets use up to 13 litres per flush. Modern high-efficiency models use 4.8 litres or less. We help you select and install water-saving toilets that meet your needs.

Toilet Clog Removal

Stubborn toilet clogs that don't respond to plunging require professional augers. We clear toilet blockages without damage to porcelain.

Bidet and Smart Toilet Installation

Modern bidets and smart toilets are increasingly popular. We handle electrical and plumbing requirements for these installations.

Faucet Repair Services

Dripping Faucet Repair

A dripping faucet usually means a worn O-ring, washer, or cartridge. Different faucet types (compression, ball, cartridge, ceramic disc) require different repair approaches. We work with all major brands.

Low Water Pressure

Reduced flow from a faucet is often caused by aerator buildup or supply line restriction. We restore full flow and pressure.

Leaking Under Sink

Leaks beneath sinks come from supply lines, P-traps, or faucet bases. We identify the source and repair it before water damages cabinets.

Faucet Replacement

Whether you're upgrading for style, function, or because of a failing unit, we install all types of faucets — kitchen, bathroom, utility, and outdoor.

Outdoor Hose Bib Repair

Frost-free hose bibs can fail due to freeze damage. We repair and replace exterior taps and add proper insulation for winter protection.

Common Tri-Cities Fixture Issues

Hard water in some Tri-Cities areas accelerates faucet cartridge wear. Mineral buildup affects fill valves in toilets too. Regular maintenance and quality replacement parts extend fixture life. We use OEM-quality parts whenever possible.

When to Repair vs Replace

Repair Makes Sense When:

  • Fixture is under 10 years old
  • It's a single component issue
  • Repair cost is under 50% of replacement
  • You're happy with the fixture's appearance and function

Replace When:

  • Fixture is over 15 years old
  • Multiple components are failing
  • You want water-saving benefits
  • Style or function no longer suits you
